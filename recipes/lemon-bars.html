Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description:

Mixing together softened butter and powdered sugar until smooth and creamy. Add flour and a pinch of salt, combining until the mixture forms a soft dough. Press this dough into the base of a lined baking pan to form a crust. Bake the crust until it's lightly golden. Meanwhile, whisk together eggs, fresh lemon juice, sugar, and a bit of flour for thickness, creating a tangy lemon filling. Pour this filling over the warm crust for a custard-like layer thats perfectly tart and sweet.

Return the pan to the oven and bake until the filling is set and lightly golden around the edges. Allow the lemon bars to cool completely in the pan, then dust with powdered sugar. Cut into squares and enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (divided)
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1.5 cups white sugar
  • 2/3 cup fresh lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix butter and powdered sugar until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add 1.5 cups of flour and salt, stirring until it forms a soft dough.
  4. Press the dough evenly into the prepared pan to form a crust.
  5. Bake the crust for 15-20 minutes, until lightly golden.
  6. While the crust bakes, whisk together eggs, white sugar, lemon juice, and remaining flour.
  7. Pour the lemon mixture over the warm crust, spreading evenly.
  8. Return to the oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, until filling is set.
  9. Allow bars to cool completely in the pan.
  10. Dust with powdered sugar, cut into squares, and enjoy!
