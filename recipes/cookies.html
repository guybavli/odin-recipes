Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description:

Creaming together softened butter and white sugar until smooth, then add a touch of molasses for a rich, slightly caramel flavor. Beat in the eggs one at a time, ensuring each is fully incorporated. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, a pinch of salt, a dash of cinnamon, and baking soda to evenly distribute the dry ingredients. Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Fold in chopped walnuts and chocolate pieces to add crunch and bursts of melted chocolate throughout.

Scoop the dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, spacing each scoop evenly, and bake in a preheated oven at 350째F (175째C) for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown but the centers are still soft. Cool on the baking sheet briefly, then transfer to a wire rack to finish cooling.

Ingredients:

  • 3.5 cups white sugar
  • 400 grams butter
  • 1 tbsp molasses
  • 4 eggs
  • 650 grams all purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp baking soda
  • 1.5 cups walnuts
  • 200 grams semi sweet baking chocolate

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (175째C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and white sugar until smooth.
  3. Add a small spoonful of molasses and blend it in well.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, mixing thoroughly after each.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, salt, cinnamon, and baking soda.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined.
  7. Fold in the chopped walnuts and chocolate pieces.
  8. Scoop the dough onto the lined baking sheet, spacing evenly.
  9. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until edges are golden and centers are soft.
  10. Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
