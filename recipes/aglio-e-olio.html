Aglio E Olio

Description:

Start by cooking pasta in a pot of boiling salted water until al dente. While the pasta cooks, gently heat olive oil in a large skillet, then add thinly sliced garlic, cooking it until fragrant and lightly golden. Sprinkle in red pepper flakes for a hint of heat. Once the pasta is done, transfer it to the skillet with the garlic oil, tossing to coat the pasta evenly. Finish with a handful of fresh parsley and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese for a simple yet flavorful dish.

Serve hot, garnished with extra Parmesan and a drizzle of olive oil if desired. Enjoy this classic, comforting Italian pasta thats quick to make and full of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 400 grams spaghetti or linguine
  • 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt (for pasta water)
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook pasta until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add sliced garlic to the skillet and cook until fragrant and lightly golden.
  4. Sprinkle in red pepper flakes and stir for a few seconds.
  5. Once the pasta is done, reserve a cup of pasta water and drain the pasta.
  6. Transfer the pasta to the skillet, tossing to coat in the garlic oil.
  7. Add a splash of reserved pasta water to help the sauce coat the pasta.
  8. Toss in fresh parsley and mix well.
  9. Serve with grated Parmesan on top, if desired.
  10. Garnish with extra olive oil or red pepper flakes, if desired, and enjoy!
